<h1>Microsoft Sales Breakdown</h1>
<p><b>Features</b></p>
<p>The first card shows us the total profit earned by Microsoft. The second card shows us the average revenue, and the last card shows us the total sales of the company.</p>
<p>You can filter the product, custmoer segmentation and year.</p>
<p>In the line chart, the sales in the first two quarters are on a declining trend, but in the third quarter they drastically increased, and it kept increasing.</p>
<p>On the weekly profit Monday has the highest profit.</p>
<p>The tree map shows the highest sales in region. Europe, Africa, Asia and Middle East has almost the same amount of sales where North America has the lowest sales</p>
<p>In the donut chart, we can see that Hardware has the highest average revenue compared to other categories.</p>
